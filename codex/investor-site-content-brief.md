# VeeVee Investor Site Content Brief

## What VeeVee Is

VeeVee is building one connected platform across two high-value fronts:

1. A consumer health companion that helps people understand benefits, ask everyday health questions, stay connected to care teams, create a personal avatar, and run simple health simulations through VeeVee Simulator(R).
2. A hospital edge AI platform that brings real-time monitoring, automated clinical documentation, continuous vigilance, and hospital-to-home engagement into one modern operating model.

The investor story should make clear that these are not two unrelated businesses. They are two sides of the same strategy:

- better health engagement for the individual
- better operational intelligence for the care system
- one data and intelligence loop from bedside to home

## Core Narrative

### Short Positioning

VeeVee is a health companion for people and an edge AI ecosystem for hospitals.

### Expanded Positioning

VeeVee helps people navigate health with more clarity, confidence, and continuity. At the same time, VeeVee helps hospitals run safer, more efficient, and more responsive care environments through edge AI infrastructure designed for real-time monitoring and action.

### Investor Framing

The platform creates value in three reinforcing ways:

- consumer engagement and recurring health utility
- hospital workflow and safety infrastructure
- a long-term intelligence layer that connects person-level engagement with care-system visibility

## Content Strategy For The Investor Site

The investor site should not read like the public website.

The public site is designed to feel calm, supportive, and easy to understand.
The investor site should feel sharper and more strategic:

- category-defining
- technically credible
- commercially disciplined
- large-market oriented

It should emphasize:

- why this market matters now
- why VeeVee can win
- why the architecture is hard to replicate
- why the consumer and hospital sides strengthen each other

### Messaging Pillars

#### 1. Hospital To Home Is The Wedge

Most systems break at transition points. VeeVee closes the gap between inpatient care, discharge, recovery, and daily life at home.

#### 2. Edge AI Is The Infrastructure Advantage

VeeVee is not just another dashboard or workflow app. It is built on a real edge compute architecture designed for low-latency, privacy-aware sensing and inference in hospitals.

#### 3. The User Relationship Compounds

On the consumer side, VeeVee aims to become a daily health companion:

- benefit discovery
- everyday questions
- care navigation
- avatar creation
- VeeVee Simulator(R)

That creates retention, trust, and a durable intelligence layer over time.

#### 4. Enterprise Value Is Measurable

For hospitals, VeeVee supports:

- new reimbursement opportunity
- labor cost reduction
- safety and risk mitigation
- better documentation workflows
- stronger post-discharge engagement

## Go-To-Market For Users

The consumer go-to-market should feel simple and progressive, not futuristic for the sake of it.

### Phase 1: Companion First

Lead with immediate utility:

- ask everyday health questions
- understand coverage and benefits
- know what to do next
- stay connected after visits

The first promise is not the avatar.
The first promise is clarity and support.

### Phase 2: Avatar Creation

Once trust is established, invite users to create their VeeVee avatar.

The avatar should be framed as:

- a more personal way to interact with your health
- a visual identity for your care journey
- the beginning of a more tailored VeeVee experience

This is not cosmetic. It is the emotional bridge into deeper engagement.

### Phase 3: VeeVee Simulator(R)

After avatar creation, guide users into VeeVee Simulator(R).

The value proposition:

- explore what-if scenarios
- understand how habits or symptoms may change next steps
- preview questions to ask
- build confidence before decisions feel urgent

The simulator is how VeeVee moves from being a helper to being an ongoing health companion.

### Consumer Funnel Summary

1. Get help now
2. Build trust
3. Create avatar
4. Try simulation
5. Return for ongoing support

## Go-To-Market For Hospitals

The hospital motion should be framed around financial value, operational value, and strategic infrastructure value.

### Entry Point

Start with a unit-level or bed-level deployment where ROI is easy to show:

- continuous vigilance
- fall-risk monitoring
- documentation assistance
- 1:1 sitter replacement support

### Expansion Motion

Expand from the bedside to the unit, then from the unit to centralized oversight and fleet operations.

### Hospital Value Narrative

VeeVee helps hospitals:

- generate new revenue through supported workflows
- reduce labor dependence in high-cost observation models
- improve response and visibility
- create a stronger bridge from bedside to home

## Technical Architecture

This section should be presented clearly on the investor site because it is part of the moat.

### Bedside Edge

**Lenovo ThinkEdge SE100**

This is the bedside edge node.

Role:

- local room-level inference
- low-latency bedside sensing
- privacy-aware processing close to the patient

Use case:

- visual monitoring
- event detection
- edge AI execution where sensitive data should stay local

### Near Edge At The Unit Level

**Lenovo ThinkEdge SE450**

This is the near-edge unit node, intended for the nursing station environment and located in the Intermediate Distribution Frame (IDF) closet.

Role:

- aggregate and orchestrate multiple bedside streams
- support unit-level inference workloads
- manage higher-throughput AI operations across a 36-bed environment
- serve as the operational bridge between bedside intelligence and centralized management

### Central Fleet Hub

**Lenovo SR650**

This is the central hub for fleet management and system-wide orchestration.

Role:

- centralized fleet oversight
- deployment control
- policy and model distribution
- operational monitoring across sites and units

### Architecture Summary

- bedside edge: Lenovo SE100
- near edge at nursing station / IDF: Lenovo SE450
- central fleet hub: Lenovo SR650

This gives VeeVee a clear three-layer architecture:

1. room-level sensing
2. unit-level orchestration
3. centralized fleet governance
