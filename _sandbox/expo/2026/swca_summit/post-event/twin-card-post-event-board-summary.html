Booth Insight

“A user mentioned that she would like to have a conversation, a real honest conversation with herself.”
  • The avatar was not only a novelty. Women described wanting multiple avatar experiences: mobile guardian and larger, private at-home reflection.
  • One attendee called it “naked truth” time: not an AI scolding her, but herself reflecting back on the choices she said she wanted to change.
  • Core insight: self-to-self accountability may land differently than a doctor, coach, or AI telling the person what to do.
  • Board takeaway: operational proof is strong; the deeper opportunity is emotional resonance and conversion polish.
Interpretation: The booth concept operated in a live expo setting; the next discussion is conversion quality.
