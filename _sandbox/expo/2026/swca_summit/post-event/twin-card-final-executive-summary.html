Financial Closeout

  • Total estimated event cost: $1,700.84 with 15% margin / overhead.
  • Cost per lead: $30.37 across 56 live Twin Card leads.
  • Cost per email sent: $36.97 across 46 sent emails.
  • Assumptions: $600 staff, $75 transport/water, $800 reusable booth kit, $3.99 tracked model spend.
  • The $800 printer/materials/roll-up/monitor kit can be reused for future booth events.

Executive Observations

  • The avatar was a powerful emotional doorway, not just a novelty.
  • Attendees described wanting honest conversations with themselves through their avatar.
  • The next conversion gap is app activation: the completed journey should leave the user with an account, their twin inside VeeVee, and a reason to return.
  • The event validated the core concept: fast, personal, visual, and memorable.
